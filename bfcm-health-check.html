Overall BFCM Readiness

1.8/5 — Critical Risk

Your site faces a critical performance crisis that will impact every single visitor during BFCM. With Largest Contentful Paint (LCP) times ranging from 12.4 to 31.25 seconds—far exceeding Google's 2.5-second threshold—you're looking at massive abandonment at every step of the customer journey.

Here's what this means in real terms: 53% of mobile users abandon sites that take longer than 3 seconds to load. At your current 26-31 second load times on product pages, you're essentially closing the door on the majority of potential customers before they even see your products. During BFCM traffic spikes when networks are congested and every millisecond counts, these delays will compound—turning a slow experience into a completely unusable one.

The conversion funnel breaks down at every stage: visitors bounce from slow-loading collection pages, those who stay can't interact with janky product detail pages (your Cumulative Layout Shift scores of 1.10-1.27 mean elements are jumping around as they try to add items to cart), and the 6.8-7.4MB page weights mean shoppers on mobile networks will experience timeouts and failed requests. You're not just losing a percentage of conversions—you're at risk of losing the vast majority of potential BFCM revenue.

Bottom line: Without immediate performance fixes, your BFCM campaign will be undermined before it begins. The good news? The fixes are achievable and high-impact—preloading hero images, deferring third-party scripts, and optimizing layout stability can move you from "critical risk" to "BFCM-ready" within 2-3 weeks.

🚫 Top 5 Conversion-Impacting Violations

  1. Insufficient Color Contrast:
    Text on imagery, secondary text, badges fail 4.5:1 ratio
    Impact: Low legibility kills CTA click-through
  2. Icon-Only Controls Lack Names:
    Filters, wishlist, social icons invisible to screen readers
    Impact: AT users can't operate key functions → drop-offs
  3. Buttons/Links Without Names:
    Card CTAs, SVG links missing aria-label or inner text
    Impact: ATC/favorite actions invisible to assistive tech
  4. Tap Targets Too Small:
    Filters, chips, pagination, social under 44×44px
    Impact: Mis-taps increase mobile friction/abandonment
  5. Poor Heading/List Structure:
    Heading skips, lists without proper li elements
    Impact: Breaks keyboard nav during deal hunting

♿ Accessibility Quick Wins (High Impact)

  1. Enable Pinch-Zoom:
    Change viewport meta to allow user scaling
    width=device-width,initial-scale=1 (remove user-scalable=no)
  2. Add Accessible Names to Icon Controls:
    <button><span class="visually-hidden">Open filters</span>{% icon %}</button>
    Apply to filters, wishlist, share, close buttons
  3. Raise Tap Targets to 44px:
    .hit-area { display:inline-flex; padding:.625rem; }
    Apply to filters, pagination, chips, social icons
  4. Fix Product Image Alt Text:
    alt: product.featured_image.alt | default: product.title
    Improves decision support when images fail/slow
  5. Fix Heading Hierarchy:
    H1 for category/product, H2 for sections, no skips
    Improves keyboard navigation of deals

⚠️ BFCM Accessibility Risk

Multiple A/AA failures on contrast, control names, tap targets, and structure will hinder promo discovery and cart progression for 15%+ of users (keyboard/assistive technology users). During peak traffic, accessibility barriers become conversion killers.
